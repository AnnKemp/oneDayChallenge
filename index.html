Summary

Food festival. Taste of Antwerp (Antwerpen Proeft) is an event held in May at Waagnatie Expo & Events in Antwerp, Belgium. It attracts visitors with a great selection of food served by more than 30 top local restaurants, Michelin-starred chefs and renowned caterers at affordable prices. The offering includes the best from international cuisines, including Belgian-French, Italian, Japanese and pan-African.
